Component Separation

One donation. Many lives. Maximize your impact through science-driven separation.

The Power of Precision

Blood isn’t just red fluid — it’s a blend of components, each vital for different treatments. Through component separation, we isolate red cells, platelets, and plasma to match each patient's unique need.

Why waste when you can target? One unit from you can serve up to 3 patients, all battling different conditions.

[ Insert image – machines / lab process ]

How It Works

  1. You donate whole blood.
  2. In our lab, a centrifuge spins it at high speed.
  3. We extract red cells, plasma, and platelets — each sent where it’s needed most.

This process is efficient, scientific, and tailored to modern medical demands.

Who Needs Separated Components?

  • Red cells: For accident victims or surgical patients
  • Plasma: For burn and clotting disorder patients
  • Platelets: For cancer or transplant patients

Why It Matters

Hospitals no longer rely on full blood bags for every case. They need *just what the patient lacks* — and that’s where you come in.

Your donation becomes flexible, efficient, and tailored to urgent needs.

It's not just giving blood. It’s giving smart.
